# Marketing Insights Report

## Key Findings

### 1. Revenue Contribution

E-Commerce generated 53% of total revenue, making it the highest-performing marketing channel.

### 2. Campaign Performance

Website Banner contributed 53% of campaign sales and outperformed other marketing channels.

### 3. Underperforming Channel

Email generated only 0.36% of total sales, indicating low campaign effectiveness.

### 4. Sales Trend

June experienced a significant decline in sales performance compared to other months.

### 5. Average Order Value

Average Order Value (AOV) was $358.27, with noticeable variation across months.

## Recommendations

1. Increase investment in high-performing channels.

2. Re-evaluate Email campaign strategy.

3. Investigate June sales decline.

4. Monitor AOV trends regularly.
